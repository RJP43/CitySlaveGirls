This website is designed with the intention to restore Nell Nelson’s instrumental work in exposing the cruel and unsanitary working conditions of Chicago’s manufacturing industry during the late nineteenth century. Nelson’s series, "City Slave Girls," was published in The Chicago Times in late July, 1888. Nelson was a pioneer of investigative reporting. By focusing on the ill-effects of industrialization, she exposed the mistreatment of the women, children, and impoverished that worked in Chicago's manufacturing industries.
