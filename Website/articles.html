The “City Slave Girls” newspaper articles as they appeared in the Chicago Times are available as PDF images on the NYU Digital Library website. The articles provided on this website are transcribed from those PDF scans. Any transcription errors are likely due to the poor quality of the images. The series includes all twenty-one articles, as well as letters received by the editor and published by The Chicago Times in response to the series.
