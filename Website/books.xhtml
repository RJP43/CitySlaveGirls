Book Publications that Directly Reference Nell Nelson's "City Slave Girls" Series

The White Slave Girls of Chicago:

This text includes a majority of the series; however, this book excludes the series’ responses that were originally published aloongside the articles. The reports are written out in plain text versus article format. The identities of the companies, employers, and employees are abbreviated. The original articles and headlines are abridged.

The White Slaves of Free America:

This text includes only portions of Nelson’s series. The author, McEnnis, incorporated the “City Slave Girls” series in his broadened exposé of the labor conditions across America’s largest cities. In this text the company names and addresses are excluded and the names of people are abbreviated.
