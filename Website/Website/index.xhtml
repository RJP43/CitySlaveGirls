This website is designed with the intention to restore Nell Nelson as an influential female journalist and pioneer of investigative reporting in the late nineteenth century. The illumination of Nelson’s “City Slave Girls” series, as a fundamental exposé of the cruel and unsanitary working conditions of industrialization, highlight Nelson’s ability to overcome the gender restraints she faced as a middle-class woman unveiling the most prominent men and successful corporations of Chicago as corrupt.
