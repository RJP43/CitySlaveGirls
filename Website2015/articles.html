The “City Slave Girls” newspaper articles as they appeared in the Chicago Daily Times are available as PDF images on the NYU Digital Library. The articles provided to the left are transcribed from the PDF scans from the above mentioned site. Any transcription errors are likely due to the poor quality of the images. Within the markup of these articles, we used the self-closing tag < unclear/ > to mark areas where the transcriber was unsure of what was written. The series includes all twenty-one articles as well as letters received by the editor and published by The Chicago Daily Times in response to the series.
